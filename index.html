Fascination

iconic and not, that have contributed
to the identity of the society in which we live.

What is the“retrò style”?
Retrò is a style that draw the charm
of the past garments,
giving temper and visibility

Our story

Fascination was born with the idea and the desire of to do rediscovering the meaning of every single garment to anyone willing to experiment, change and know
a part of our story. Our store dedicates to the 20th Century fashion, whose his decades are often associated exclusively to outfit victims of stereotypes, and Fascination
commits to remedy at this too: uproot the clichés and bring back the authenticity of the extraordinary work of people lived before us, without which we couldn’t convey
to you the charm of retro'.
The choice of the two alternatives, tailoring and prêt-à-porter, is mainly due to the care towards the preoccupations of our customers.


Tailoring offers several advantages:

  • the customer can decide the fabric he likes more
  • a garment that fits perfectly to the body offers more confidence
    in the self-esteem of the person;
  • the personal advices by the tailorist are very useful:
    our taylor has as objective the completely satisfaction
    of the person who dresses, removing any doubts and insecurity
    with the magic touch of her sewing scissors!
